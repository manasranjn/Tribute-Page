A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

Father of India Missile Programmed Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam is the 11th president of India. His full name is Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam. Abdul kalam is the best president of India till date. He is best known as the “missile man” to every common Indian. He was born on October 15, 1931 at Dhanush Kothi in the temple town Rameswaram in Tamil Nadu. His father Jainulabdeen was boat owner and imam of local mosque. His mother Ashiamma was a housewife. He was born in a poor Family, but he was a brilliant child.



He completed his school education from Schwartz Higher Secondary School at Ramanathapuram. Dr. Kalam passed the B.Sc. examination from saint Joseph Collage, Tiruchirappalli. He qualified as an aeronautical Engineer from Madras Technical Institute. After graduation from Madras Institute of Technology he started his career as a scientist DRDO in 1958. He worked on the designing, development and management of indigenous satellite rocket. And after some time, satellite ‘Rohini’ was successfully launched.
